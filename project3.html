Dentro il Mare

"Dentro il Mare" is an educational project born from the collaboration between classes 2M and 3M of I.C. Cassola in Cecina and the Institute of Mechanical Intelligence at Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna in Pisa.

The students designed an immersive virtual reality experience focused on the marine world, exploring topics such as marine biology and the underwater environment.

The project included both theoretical lessons on digital technologies and practical workshops, during which students conducted research, selected content, and created materials aimed at raising awareness about marine conservation.



The project was structured in two main phases: Theoretical lessons to deepen students' knowledge of the digital world
Hands-on activities to apply and experiment with what they had learned



The students:
Conducted thematic research on the marine environment
Selected information and images to support their exploration
